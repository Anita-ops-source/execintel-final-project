About BriefIQ AI

BriefIQ is a lightweight executive assistant that turns messy business inputs into decision-ready outputs. Instead of forcing leaders to read long updates, it produces a short summary, key risks, and clear next actions. The goal is to help teams align faster, reduce missed issues, and make decisions with better context. This demo shows how the concept could work in a real workplace setting.
