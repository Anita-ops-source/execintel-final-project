How It Works

ExecIntel turns unstructured executive communications into a board-ready brief. Designed for pilot readiness: simple workflow, clear output, and easy stakeholder review.

1) Ingest

Upload audio/video or paste a transcript. ExecIntel extracts the key statements and context.

2) Summarize

Generates an executive summary, major drivers, and financial/operational highlights.

3) Risk + Actions

Flags risks and produces department-level action items (Finance, Ops, Sales, Supply Chain).

4) Validate Claims

Cross-checks market claims and assumptions using lightweight web grounding (where available).

5) Share

Outputs a clean “Intelligence Brief” format that is easy for stakeholders to review.

6) Iterate

Refine based on feedback, update inputs, and maintain consistent formatting over time.
